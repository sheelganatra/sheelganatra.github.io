Old Exams

The following exams were given in previous incarnations of Math 51. The topics covered in Math 51 change somewhat from year to year -- there may be material on the exam for this quarter which was NOT covered on previous exams (and vice versa). Additionally material covered on a given midterm of a given course may not exactly correlate to the material covered in Math 51 this quarter, so please bear this in mind when working these examples. Also keep in mind that the midterm exams are not of a uniform length, since different courses set different time limits (60 minutes, 90 minutes, 120 minutes, etc).

When reviewing these exams you should attempt to solve the problems on your own without looking at the included solutions. Working the problems with the solutions at hand will do you no good at all.
